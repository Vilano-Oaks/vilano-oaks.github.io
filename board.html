  • Open Meetings!

    Vilano Oaks HOA meetings are open to the membership. Homeowners are notified in advance of meetings by email and/or mail post card. It is important that you keep us up to date with yoour current email address by sending an email to the current secretary (contact information found below). Since the creation of the virtual ARC process, in-person meetings of the board are called when agenda warrants, or at least quarterly. Unless there is a scheduling issue, they are held at at the South Ponte Vedra Civic Association. Agendas, minutes, and other delivereables are published / archived below. If you see something that interests or concerns you, we want to hear from you! Feel free to contact any member of the Board personally or via the eMail links provided on this page.

Vilano Oaks Homeowners Association

An active Homeowners Association is in place to promote community unity, review and revise community architectural and land use standards per input from the community at large. For the maintenance of the common areas, street lights and insurance, residents currently pay a $230.00 annual homeowners fee. Neighborhood business is managed by the Homeowners Association Board of Directors. Directors are members of the community and serve on a volunteer basis. The Board consists of members directly elected by the residents to three year terms.
