Vilano Oaks

Life IS better by the beach!

Nestled between the Atlantic Ocean and the Intracoastal Waterway lies a neighborhood where families know each other and beautiful homes and gardens are surrounded by ocean breezes.

Located just west of the A1A Coastal Highway, Vilano Oaks is close to the conveniences of Saint Augustine and Jacksonville, while still enjoying all the benefits of a secluded beach setting. Its residents have a strong sense of community and get together regularly for social events.

Since its establishment in 2002, Vilano Oaks has preserved its integrity as a quality neighborhood through the efforts of an active Homeowners Association and conscientious neighbors. As a result, residents enjoy strong property values and a safe environment for families.

For more information about our neighborhood, we invite you to browse our website. See why it is more than beautiful homes that makes Vilano Oaks a special place to live.

Vilano Oaks Homeowners Association

An active Homeowners Association is in place to promote community unity, review and revise community architectural and land use standards per input from the community at large. For the maintenance of the common areas, street lights and insurance, residents currently pay a $230.00 annual homeowners fee. Neighborhood business is managed by the Homeowners Association Board of Directors. Directors are members of the community and serve on a volunteer basis.
